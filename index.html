About my program: Business Technology Management (BTM)

The Business Technology Management (BTM) program at Toronto Metropolitan University helps students develop analytical and problem-solving skills to create business solutions using Information and Communication Technologies (ICT). This program provides flexibility for students — allowing them to pursue a wide range of careers in business and management. The BTM program is accredited by the Information Technology Association of Canada (ITAC), which ensures that the education meets the highest quality standards, preparing graduates to make a real impact in their chosen fields.

Course I Found Most Useful: ITM 550 - Advanced e-Business

The course I found most useful in the BTM program was ITM 550: Advanced e-Business. It was incredibly hands-on and gave me practical experience in building a fully functional e-commerce website using Shopify. We did everything from deciding the font size of navigation menus to editing product photos. One of the most valuable aspects was ensuring that the website was accessibility-friendly, meaning it was designed to be usable by people with disabilities such as deafness, blindness, and dyslexia. This project helped me understand the entire process of creating an e-commerce site and ensuring it meets real-world requirements.
