About my program: Business Technology Management (BTM)

The Business Technology Management (BTM) program at Toronto Metropolitan University teaches students how to solve problems and create business solutions using technology. It offers a lot of flexibility, letting students choose various career paths in business and technology.

Course I Found Most Useful: ITM 550 - Advanced e-Business

The course I found most useful in the BTM program was ITM 550: Advanced e-Business. It was incredibly hands-on and gave me practical experience in building an e-commerce website using Shopify. We did everything from deciding the font size of navigation menus to editing product photos. The website was designed to be accessibility friendly, so it is usable by people with disabilities. This project helped me understand the entire process of creating an e-commerce site and ensuring it meets real-world requirements.
